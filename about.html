Transforming Logistics Since 2006

SSLW is a leading integrated logistics and 3PL service provider delivering scalable, technology-driven supply chain solutions across the World. Established as a regional courier company, SSLW has evolved into a nationwide logistics partner with a strong presence across multiple industries, supporting multinational corporations, government departments, and fast-growing enterprises.

With a robust network of 70+ branches and more than 10,000 delivery touchpoints, SSLW specializes in managing complex supply chains across transportation, warehousing, and distribution ecosystems. The company combines operational expertise with technology-enabled logistics models to ensure real-time visibility, efficient movement of goods, and consistent service reliability across the country.

With expertise spanning PTL, FTL, warehousing, last mile delivery, sourcing, and integrated logistics, SSLW enables businesses to optimize supply chain costs while improving efficiency and scalability. Backed by experienced leadership and 24/7 operational support, SSLW continues to help organizations focus on growth while managing end-to-end logistics operations seamlessly.
